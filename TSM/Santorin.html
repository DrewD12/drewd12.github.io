Santorin started playing video games when he had the age of 4-5 years old. He was studying Mathematics, Physics and Social Studies at the Greve Gymnasium High School, but put the studies on hold when moving to USA to play profesionally, but plan to finish it at one time.[1] Before he went to League of Legends he was a football player. He was also a very good Counterstrike gamer. The meaning of his name is nothing special he asked a Swedish friend and he came up with the name "Santorin" and since then he is using that name. His League of Legends career started when he was searching for something new since he got tired of Counterstrike. He started in the middle of season 2. As of October 14th, 2015, Santorin [2] with the intent to remain in the scene while not playing competitively. “I have enjoyed my time with TSM but we have now decided to part ways. Everyone has been nice to me and Andy has always done his best to help me out with everything. I will most likely not be playing competitive League Of Legends anymore but I will try to make full time streaming work while studying on the side.”

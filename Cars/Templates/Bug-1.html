Backgroud:
The Bugatti Veyron Super Sport, or SS for short, is one of the most well known Bugatti models out there. That's because it is the fastest street legal car recognized by Guinness World Records. However, it is very rare because it has a huge price tag: $2,250,000. Figuring the large cost not many people have one. Some celebrities however have invested in such a car such as, Simon Cowell, Jay-z as well was Tom Brady.

Stats:
Speed: 430.9 km/h (267.7 mph)
0-60mph: 2.46 seconds
Engine: 8.0 liter W16, 64v 2xDOHC quad-turbocharged petrol engine.

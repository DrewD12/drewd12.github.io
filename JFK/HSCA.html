HSCA

HSCA stands for, United States House Select Committee on Assassinations. This committee was established in 1976 and was created to solve the assassinations of both the 35th president John F. Kennedy and African Activist Martin Luther King Jr.

The HSCA beilved that the other orginizations did their best on investigations but lacked intrest in the possiblity of a conspiracy. With available evidence the HSCA finally concluded that the assassination was a conspiracy.

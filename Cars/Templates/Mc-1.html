Backgroud:
The McLaren P1 is the successor of the McLaren F1 using hybrid engines and a formula one design. The P1 started to be relased in Octover 2013 and was sold out by Novemeber of the same year. The car costing $1.15 million only had 375 units made.

Stats:
Speed: 350 km/h (217 mph)
0-60mph: 2.9 sec seconds
Engine: 3.8 L twin-turbo M838TQ V8
Fun Fact: in 2015, Mclaren unveild at track only version that could only be sold to an owner of the original P1.

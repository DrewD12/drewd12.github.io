Backgroud:
The Bugatti Grand Sport Vitese is Bugatti's roadster, or convertable. Bugatti was able to create yet another world record setter, but this time without a roof. This car allows you to drive and hear the powerful engine and super chargers.

Stats:
Speed: 410 km/h (254.762mph)
0-60mph: 2.6 sec seconds
Engine: 7.9 liter 16-cylinder
Fun Fact: The two air scopes are not only for the engine air intake, but also are apart of the rollover protection system.
